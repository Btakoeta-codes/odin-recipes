Fufu-and-eru

Fufu-and-eru delicacy

Eru is a popular soup in Cameroon that is enjoyed by many people across the country. The soup is made from a variety of ingredients including leafy greens, meat, and spices.

It is often served with a side of fufu, which is a starchy side dish made from cassava, plantains, or yams.
